# FINRA Compliance Rules

## Rule 3110 - Supervision
1. Each member shall establish and maintain a system to supervise the activities of each associated person.
2. Supervisory procedures must be reasonably designed to achieve compliance with applicable securities laws.
3. Regular review and updating of supervisory procedures is required.

## Rule 3120 - Supervisory Control System
1. Members must establish, maintain, and enforce written supervisory control policies and procedures.
2. Annual review of business lines and locations is required.
3. Summary report must be provided to senior management annually.

## Rule 5310 - Best Execution and Interpositioning
1. Orders must be executed at the most favorable terms reasonably available under the circumstances.
2. Regular and rigorous review of execution quality is required.
3. Interpositioning between customer and market is prohibited unless beneficial to customer.

## Rule 2090 - Know Your Customer
1. Members must use reasonable diligence to know and retain essential facts concerning every customer.
2. Facts include investment profile, risk tolerance, and investment objectives.
3. Information must be updated regularly.

## Rule 2111 - Suitability
1. Recommendations must be suitable based on customer's investment profile.
2. Three main suitability obligations: reasonable basis, customer-specific, quantitative.
3. Institutional customers may have different suitability standards.

## Rule 6130 - Order Audit Trail System (OATS)
1. Members must record and report order information to OATS.
2. Clock synchronization within 50 milliseconds of NIST time is required.
3. Electronic order handling requires detailed audit trail.

## Market Making Rules
- Minimum quote size requirements apply
- Maximum spread limitations in certain securities
- Continuous two-sided quoting obligations

## Key Risk Indicators
- Maximum daily loss limit per customer: $50,000 (retail)
- Maximum concentration in single security: 25% of portfolio
- Pattern day trader minimum equity: $25,000
- Margin maintenance requirement: 25% (long), 30% (short)

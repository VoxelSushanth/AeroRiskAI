# MiFID II Compliance Rules

## Article 16 - Algorithmic Trading Requirements
1. Investment firms engaging in algorithmic trading shall have in place effective systems and risk controls.
2. Systems shall ensure trading is conducted in an orderly manner and avoid creating disorderly market conditions.
3. Firms must have business continuity arrangements to ensure maintenance of systems and prevent technical failures.

## Article 17 - Market Making Obligations
1. Algorithmic traders providing liquidity on a regular basis shall be subject to requirements to ensure adequate systems and controls.
2. Market makers must quote prices at which they are willing to trade for most of the trading day.
3. Exemptions may apply for periods of severe market stress.

## Article 48 - Trading Venue Requirements
1. Trading venues shall have in place circuit breakers that temporarily or permanently halt trading.
2. Circuit breakers shall be triggered by significant price movements within short time periods.
3. Parameters shall be calibrated based on liquidity, volatility, and market conditions.

## Article 25 - Client Order Handling
1. Firms must execute orders on terms most favorable to the client.
2. Best execution policies must be disclosed to clients.
3. Regular monitoring of execution quality is required.

## Article 24 - Product Governance
1. Manufacturers must identify target markets for each financial instrument.
2. Distribution strategies must be consistent with identified target markets.
3. Regular review of products against target market assumptions is required.

## Key Risk Indicators
- Maximum order-to-trade ratio: 10:1
- Maximum message rate per second: 1000
- Price collar deviation: ±10% from reference price
- Minimum resting time for orders: 500ms (where applicable)

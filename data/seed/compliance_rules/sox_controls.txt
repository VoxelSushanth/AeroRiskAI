# Sarbanes-Oxley (SOX) Compliance Controls

## Section 302 - Corporate Responsibility for Financial Reports
1. CEOs and CFOs must certify the accuracy of financial reports.
2. Certifications must confirm disclosure controls and procedures are effective.
3. Internal control over financial reporting must be evaluated quarterly.

## Section 404 - Management Assessment of Internal Controls
1. Management must assess effectiveness of internal control structure annually.
2. External auditor must attest to management's assessment.
3. Deficiencies must be classified as: Control Deficiency, Significant Deficiency, or Material Weakness.

## Section 409 - Real-Time Issuer Disclosures
1. Material changes in financial condition must be disclosed on a rapid and current basis.
2. Disclosure timeline: Within 4 business days of triggering event.
3. Electronic filing via EDGAR system required.

## Section 802 - Criminal Penalties for Document Destruction
1. Altering, destroying, or falsifying records is prohibited.
2. Audit workpapers must be retained for 7 years.
3. Whistleblower protections apply.

## Key IT General Controls (ITGC)

### Access Controls
- Unique user identification required
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Periodic access reviews (quarterly minimum)
- Immediate deprovisioning upon termination
- Multi-factor authentication for privileged access

### Change Management
- Formal change request process
- Segregation of duties (development vs production)
- Testing requirements before deployment
- Approval workflow for production changes
- Rollback procedures documented

### Backup and Recovery
- Daily incremental backups
- Weekly full backups
- Off-site storage of backup media
- Quarterly recovery testing
- Recovery Time Objective (RTO): <4 hours
- Recovery Point Objective (RPO): <1 hour

### System Monitoring
- Continuous monitoring of critical systems
- Alert thresholds defined and documented
- Incident response procedures established
- Log retention: minimum 7 years
- Real-time alerting for critical events

## Trading-Specific SOX Controls

### Trade Capture and Validation
- All trades must be captured within 15 minutes of execution
- Automated validation against market prices
- Exception reporting for out-of-range trades
- Dual approval for manual adjustments

### Position Reconciliation
- Daily position reconciliation with custodians
- Tolerance limits: $10,000 or 1% of position
- Unreconciled items escalated within 24 hours
- Monthly sign-off by finance team

### Valuation Controls
- Independent price verification daily
- Hierarchy of pricing sources defined
- Fair value calculations documented
- Model validation annually

## Audit Trail Requirements
- Immutable transaction logs
- Timestamp precision: milliseconds
- User action logging (who, what, when)
- System state snapshots at EOD
- Tamper-evident storage
